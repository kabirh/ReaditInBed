About

Hey there,

I made RnB last summer while learning to code. I'd browse reddit while in bed giggling like an idiot, and my girlfriend (now fiance:) would ask me what I was laughing at. She'd look over at my screen and start giggling like an idiot too. Before long, we'd browse reddit in bed every night.

We found all the tapping around tedious when all we wanted to do was view the images and the highest comment though.. So a few weekends of learning javascript later and we had the first version. A year later and you're enjoying the result of lots of tinkering as I learned to cobble things together out of code. It's still far from perfect, but it's better than nothing. Hope you find it as useful as we do.

Enjoy,
Kabir
